Profile

I am a student at the University of Toronto pursuing a Bachelor of Commerce with a specialization in finance and economics. Prior to U of T, I attended high school in Vancouver where I was a member of the Investment Club, Varsity Rowing Team, and Discovery 10. I am interested in finance and have completed several related internships in private equity and corporate development. I also spend my free time researching companies to invest in.

Outside of the classroom, I am a part of RCIF, a student-run investment fund, as VP Operations, Investa Insights, an equity research club, as a Junior Portfolio Manager, and RCFTA, a FinTech club as a Finance Director. In my spare time, I enjoy cycling, traveling, reading, exploring Toronto, and spending time with friends!
